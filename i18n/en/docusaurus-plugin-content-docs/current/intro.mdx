# Welcome to the Centuply Help Center! 🎉

Here you will find the main guides you need to create, edit, manage, and publish your AI website with Centuply.

## What is Centuply?

Centuply is an **AI Website Builder** that helps you create professional websites with artificial intelligence.

## Get started in 5 minutes

1. Open the app → [app.centuply.com/app](https://app.centuply.com/app)
2. Describe your business in simple words
3. Let AI create your website
4. Edit it with the Visual Editor
5. Publish it when you are ready

[→ Go to App](https://app.centuply.com/app)
