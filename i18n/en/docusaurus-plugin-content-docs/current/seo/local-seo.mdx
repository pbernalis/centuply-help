# Local SEO: location and service areas

Local SEO helps your business appear better when customers search for services in a specific area.

If you have a physical store, office, clinic, studio, or you serve specific areas, Local SEO is very important.

## When to use this guide

Use this guide when you want to:

- set the business city correctly,
- add service areas,
- improve pages for local searches,
- write better SEO titles and descriptions,
- help customers find you more easily,
- prepare the website before publishing.

## What is Local SEO?

Local SEO is the optimization of your website for searches related to location.

Examples of searches:

- hair salon in Piraeus,
- dentist in Keratsini,
- gym in Nikaia,
- wedding photographer in Athens,
- pet grooming in the southern suburbs.

When your website clearly mentions your area and services, you help both search engines and visitors understand where you operate.

## Step 1 — Open SEO tools

Open the Centuply app, select your website, and open **SEO**.

If there is a dedicated **Local SEO** section, open it.

## Step 2 — Check basic business details

Make sure the basic details are correct:

- business name,
- city,
- area,
- address, if available,
- phone number,
- email,
- opening hours,
- main services.

These details should be consistent across the website.

## Step 3 — Add service areas

If you do not serve only one physical location but work across several areas, add them clearly.

Examples:

- Piraeus,
- Keratsini,
- Nikaia,
- Korydallos,
- Athens,
- southern suburbs,
- northern suburbs.

Do not add areas you do not actually serve.

## Step 4 — Improve SEO titles

A good local SEO title combines service and location.

Examples:

> Hair Salon in Piraeus | Haircuts, Color and Styling

> Dental Clinic in Keratsini | Cleaning and Cosmetic Dentistry

> Personal Trainer in Nikaia | Personal Workout Programs

Keep the title clear and natural.

## Step 5 — Write local meta descriptions

The meta description should explain what you offer and where.

Example:

> Professional hair salon services in Piraeus for haircuts, color, styling, and hair care. Book your appointment online.

A good description helps visitors quickly understand whether the page is relevant to them.

## Step 6 — Add location to page content

Do not include the city only in the SEO title.

Add the location naturally inside the page content too.

Example:

> Our hair salon in Piraeus offers haircut, color, and styling services for women and men.

The text should sound natural, not overloaded with repeated keywords.

## Step 7 — Check the Contact page

The Contact page is very important for local SEO.

Make sure it includes:

- business name,
- address,
- phone number,
- email,
- map, if there is a physical location,
- opening hours,
- contact form or booking option.

## Step 8 — Use AI for local suggestions

You can ask the AI Assistant to help.

Examples:

> Suggest an SEO title for a hair salon in Piraeus.

> Write a meta description for a clinic in Keratsini.

> Improve this text for local SEO.

> Suggest service areas for this business.

Always check that the information is accurate.

## Step 9 — Check consistency

Before publishing, check that the same location appears correctly everywhere:

- Home page,
- Contact page,
- Services page,
- footer,
- SEO fields,
- forms,
- booking,
- Google Business Profile, if available.

Consistency helps both visitors and search engines.

## Best practices

- Use a real location.
- Do not add areas you do not serve.
- Write naturally, not mechanically.
- Combine service + area.
- Keep phone numbers and addresses correct.
- Add a map if you have a physical location.
- Check mobile, because many local customers search from their phone.

## Frequently asked questions

### Do I need Local SEO if my business is online?

If you serve specific areas, yes. If you are fully online without geographic limits, Local SEO is less important.

### Should I add an address?

If you have a physical place that receives customers, yes. If you work by appointment or remotely, you can provide a service area.

### Can I add many areas?

Yes, but only if you actually serve them.

### Should I repeat the city all the time?

No. Write naturally. Too much repetition makes the copy worse.

### Can AI help with Local SEO?

Yes. It can suggest titles, descriptions, and copy, but you must check accuracy.
