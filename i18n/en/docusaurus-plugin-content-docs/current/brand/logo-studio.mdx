# Logo Studio

Logo Studio helps you create and review logo ideas for your website.

A logo is an important part of your brand. It appears in the header, footer, browser previews, and sometimes in social sharing or business materials.

## When to use Logo Studio

Use Logo Studio when you want to:

- create a first logo idea,
- test different brand directions,
- generate logo options,
- choose a logo for the website header,
- improve the visual identity of the website.

## Step 1 — Open Logo Studio

Open your website project in Centuply and go to **Brand**, **Logo**, or **Logo Studio**.

## Step 2 — Describe the brand

Add a short description of the business and the style you want.

Examples:

- modern and minimal,
- premium and elegant,
- friendly and local,
- professional and clean,
- bold and dynamic.

## Step 3 — Generate logo ideas

Generate a few logo options and review them carefully.

Do not choose only the first result. Compare the options and check which one fits the business best.

## Step 4 — Preview on the website

A logo should be tested inside the website layout.

Check:

- header,
- footer,
- mobile view,
- dark backgrounds,
- light backgrounds,
- small size.

## Step 5 — Choose and save

When you find the best option, save it and apply it to the website.

Then check the site again to make sure the logo looks professional.

## Best practices

- Keep the logo simple.
- Avoid too much detail.
- Check readability in small sizes.
- Make sure it matches the brand tone.
- Test it on desktop and mobile.
- Do not use a logo that looks unclear or crowded.

## Frequently asked questions

### Do I need a logo before creating a website?

No. You can start without a logo and add one later.

### Can I change the logo later?

Yes. You can update the logo when your brand direction changes.

### Should the logo be very detailed?

No. Simple logos usually work better online, especially in headers and mobile views.
