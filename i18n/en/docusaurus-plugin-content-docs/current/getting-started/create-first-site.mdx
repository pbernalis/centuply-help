# Create your first website with AI

In this guide, you will learn how to create your first website with Centuply, without needing technical knowledge or design experience.

Centuply helps you describe your business in simple words and gives you a ready website that you can later edit, improve, and publish.

## When to use this guide

Use this guide when:

- you are using Centuply for the first time,
- you want to create a new website,
- you do not know where to start,
- you want a strong first professional result quickly,
- you want to understand how AI website generation works.

## What you need before you start

Before creating your website, it helps to have:

- your business name,
- your city or service area,
- your business type,
- your main services or products,
- a phone number or email address,
- 2-3 images, if available.

You do not need to have everything perfect from the beginning. You can change it later in the editor.

## Step 1 — Open the Centuply app

Go to the Centuply app:

[Open Centuply App](https://app.centuply.com/app)

Sign in to your account and start creating a new website.

## Step 2 — Describe your business

In the first step, describe the website you want in simple words.

Example:

> I want a professional website for a hair salon in Piraeus. I want it to show services, prices, photos, contact details, and a booking button.

You can write naturally, as if you were speaking to a collaborator.

## Step 3 — Fill in the basic details

Depending on the flow you use, Centuply may ask for:

- business name,
- business category,
- location,
- main services,
- website style,
- language,
- whether you want a form, booking button, or contact details.

The clearer your information is, the better the first result will be.

## Step 4 — Continue to payment and generation

When you complete the details, continue to the payment step.

After confirmation, Centuply starts creating your website. The AI prepares:

- pages,
- copy,
- structure,
- sections,
- basic SEO,
- initial design.

## Step 5 — Review the first result

When generation is complete, you will see your website in preview.

At this stage, everything does not need to be perfect. The goal is to have a strong first base.

First check:

- if the business name is correct,
- if the main services are shown correctly,
- if the homepage makes sense,
- if basic contact details are included,
- if the style fits the business.

## Step 6 — Make your first edits

After the first result, you can open the editor and improve the website.

Common first edits include:

- changing the homepage headline,
- correcting copy,
- adding or changing images,
- changing colors,
- adding a new page,
- enabling a contact form,
- checking the contact or booking button.

## Step 7 — Use the AI Assistant

Inside the editor, you can ask the Centuply Assistant to improve parts of the website.

Examples:

> Make the hero section more premium.

> Write a shorter description for the services.

> Make the contact button stronger.

> Suggest a better headline for the homepage.

The important part is that Centuply shows you a preview first. Changes are not applied unless you click Apply.

## Step 8 — Check before publishing

Before publishing, check:

- the homepage,
- the contact page,
- phone and email details,
- links,
- mobile appearance,
- basic SEO,
- whether you have a domain or temporary preview link.

You can also use Site Health to check for issues before publishing.

## Step 9 — Publish the website

When you are ready, click Publish.

After publishing, your website becomes available online. If you have your own domain, you can connect it from the Domains section.

## Frequently asked questions

### Can I change the website after generation?

Yes. The first result is your starting point. You can change copy, images, colors, pages, and sections in the editor.

### Do I need technical knowledge?

No. Centuply is designed so you can create and edit a website without code.

### Do I need ready-made photos?

Not necessarily. If you have your own photos, you can upload them. If not, you can start with the initial result and add photos later.

### Can I use my own domain?

Yes. After creating your website, you can connect your domain from the Domains section.

### What if I do not like the first result?

You can edit it with the editor and ask the AI Assistant for improvements. The main strength of Centuply is that you are not stuck with the first result.
